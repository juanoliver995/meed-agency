My Biography

Pampa is a dj producer born in General Pico and based in the province of Córdoba.

She is characterized with her musical tendency to techno that along with incredible empathy with the public has led her to perform in places like : La Fábrica, La Estacion, Crobar, Roswell Festival, Terra Furia, Basement among others.

Her talent led her to perform from north to south, and from east to west in cities such as: Ushuaia, Chubut, Neuquén, Mar Del Plata, La Pampa, Buenos Aires, Mendoza, San Luis, Santa Fe, San Juan, La Rioja, Catamarca, Tucumán, Corrientes, Chaco, Salta, Jujuy.

He shared booths with artists such as: Adam Beyer, Lily Palmer, Marc Houle, Julian Jeweill, Victor Ruiz, Alex Stein, Nastia, Matrixman, Fabio Florido, Ann Clue, Inellea, Denis Hortvat among others.

This young artist promises to immerse the ears of her listeners in a dark and intense atmosphere, making each of her presentations a spectacle to appreciate.
